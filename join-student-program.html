After you graduate, advance your career with a VCA Internship.

Whether you are thinking of specializing, going into ER or general practice, or simply want a bit more mentorship or confidence, there is no better way to become a better doctor, faster, than through a VCA internship.

  • World’s largest provider of post-graduate veterinary training.
  • More than 160 rotating internships.
  • Over 30 hospital internship locations across North America.

VCA Academy Mentorship Program for Continued Growth

  • Personalized One-on-One Mentorship
  • Learning at Your Own Pace
  • Interactive and Collaborative Learning Environments

This 12-week Primary Care Mentorship program has three areas of focus: surgery, dentistry, and medical appointments. The six-month ER Mentorship program includes didactic training and a two-day hands-on ER Skills workshop.

Certificate Training Programs
