Veterinary Student Externship Program

Want a supportive culture to learn and thrive? Want to access the best and brightest talent working in veterinary medicine?

VCA’s Externship Program provides currently enrolled Doctor of Veterinary Medicine students, Veterinary Technician students, and Veterinary Assistant students with valuable, real-world experience by taking what you’ve studied in the classroom and bringing it to the hospital.

Search "externships" at VCAcareers.com to find current openings.

DVM Summer Experience

Calling all future vets? Get ahead over summer break with real experience.

Are you a current DVM student in an accredited veterinary university and available to work eight weeks over the summer?

If so, our paid veterinary summer program is for you!

  • Work alongside experienced veterinary medical teams.
  • Work like a doctor: write a case report on a patient of your choosing and receive expert feedback.
  • Practice examination skills, assist in anesthesia, surgery, radiology, and more*. *Can vary based on State Veterinary Practice Acts.

Interested?

Applications open in January. The DVM Summer Experience is active from April to September.
