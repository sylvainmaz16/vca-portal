Our Cultural Values

Our cultural values guide who we are and how we work. They're also our driving force in supporting you, the future of veterinary medicine.

As a learning organization, we want all our people to stay curious, seek development, and grow throughout their careers. That’s why we’ve built this site: to instill that cultural value in our students and help you on your way to starting your career.

Our VCA network spans general practice, specialty, emergency, and urgent care. As a student, you can tap into that vast expertise through our internship, externship, and summer programs. Start your career being mentored by the top medical professionals in the country.

We know how challenging becoming a veterinary medical professional can be—we’ve been there! And our community is here to support and encourage you, and offer a hand across the finish line.

Great patient care is built on trust. We believe in you to take every learning opportunity to be the best veterinary professional you can be. And you can believe in us to provide support and training to help shape your career.

We want learning to be fun. We want your career in this profession to be fun. And, we also know just how stressful veterinary medicine can be. Through nurturing unique personalities and practice styles, and through unshakable optimism, we unleash your power to keep the joy at the heart of what you do — now as a student and into your future career.

This website is brought to you by WOOF University and VCA’s Knowledge & Learning team. We support our Associates, students, and the profession by developing custom training programs—on-the-job development to help you learn, grow, and succeed.
