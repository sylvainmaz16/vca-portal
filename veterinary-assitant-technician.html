VA/VT Training Programs

A precursor to the VCA Technician & Assistant Career Development Program, the NVAD Program provides essential online and on-the-job training activities to prepare you for your new role in your hospital.

Coordinated, quality online and on-the-job training for credentialed veterinary technicians. A precursor to the VCA Technician & Assistant Career Development Program.

A foundational program for technician and assistant leaders in the hospital. Topics include being a patient advocate, creating goals, promoting a patient safety culture, leading teams, and time management.

Provides the tools, resources, and individualized learning opportunities to support talent development and accelerated career growth.

A series of online training sessions to aid learners in passing the Veterinary Technician National Exam (VTNE).

Learn veterinary medical transcription, the importance of the medical record, and key terminology—everything from professional descriptors to body systems.

Patient and Associate safety is our goal every time we enter the radiology suite. This curriculum contains 10 online courses that cover hands-free techniques to use with your canine and feline patients.

Certificate Training Programs

Everything required for Veterinarians and Technicians to effectively care for our rabbit patients and clients. Includes extensive online training in topics covering handling, husbandry, preventive and wellness care, clinical skills, and surgery.

Veterinary Technicians and Assistants will gain competency and an understanding of the anesthetic process through RACE-approved online courses, Anesthesia Study Guide, case scenarios, on-the-job assessments, and coaching.
