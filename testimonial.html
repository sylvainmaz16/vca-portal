What Our Students Have to Say

Kate Walsh DVM, Former VCA Intern

“Absolutely do it. Whatever you’ve heard that may be pros or cons, don’t even worry about it. It’s the best decision for you. You’re going to grow as a person. You’re going to improve your medicine. You’re going to be confident when you come out of the internship.”

Peter Cho DVM, Former VCA Intern

“It’s in the best interest of the pet just to have that extra training. It’s definitely worth it. I wouldn’t change it for a thing.”

Kya Degarmo DVM, Former VCA Intern

"Coming out of vet school, there's no way that I was equipped enough to go an run an ER. Coming out of an internship, I feel like I could... The support you get from this hospital and VCA—it’s nothing like I was expecting… It’s very patient driven. It’s very doctor-care driven. They really take care of us, whether you’re an intern or a specialist. The best thing about this internship is they have prioritized our learning.”

Anonymus Former Veterinary Technician Student Extern

“It was difficult piecing all of my studies together until I got to have this hands-on experience. It really helped me to understand and remember. I’ve walked away learning new things as well”

Anonymus Former DVM Student Extern

“This externship was an amazing experience. I felt like I was able to really step into the role of being the doctor on the case, not just watch from the sidelines.”

Anonymus Former Veterinary Assistant Student Extern

“I learned a lot doing hands on and learning from mentors who explained skills to me step by step clearly. They had very good patience with me and understood I was there to learn.”
